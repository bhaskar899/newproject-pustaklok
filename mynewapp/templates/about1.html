India That Is Bharat

"India, That Is Bharat: Coloniality, Civilisation, Constitution" is a book by J. Sai Deepak that explores
the impact of European colonialism on India's identity and constitutional framework.

The book, the first in a trilogy, argues that the concept of "coloniality," particularly its religious and
racial roots, has significantly shaped India's understanding of itself and its relationship with its Indic civilization.
