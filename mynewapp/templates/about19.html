That Night

That Night by Nidhi Upadhyay is a gripping psychological thriller that explores the dark consequences of a prank gone horribly wrong. It follows four college friends—Natasha, Riya, Anjali, and Katherine—whose lives take a dark turn after the death of their hostel mate, Sania.

Two decades later, secrets start resurfacing when the friends receive anonymous threats hinting that someone knows what really happened that night. With fear gripping their lives, they are forced to come together again to confront the truth they tried so hard to bury.
