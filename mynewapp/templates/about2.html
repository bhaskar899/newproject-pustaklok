The History Of Ancient And Early Medieval India


Ancient and medieval book theory, while not explicitly formalized as a discipline like modern literary theory, evolved from practical concerns about preserving and transmitting texts.

Key developments include the shift from oral traditions to written texts, the codification of knowledge in various formats (scrolls, codices), and the emergence of different approaches to textual interpretation. These approaches were shaped by philosophical and religious contexts, as well as the social and political uses of books.
